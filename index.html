About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment hub focused on digital, cyber, and technology risk roles. We bridge the gap between academic knowledge and applied practice.

Through deep collaboration with universities and hands-on industry projects, we prepare practitioners to tackle complex risk challenges — from AI governance to critical infrastructure protection.

Our Services

Academy

Training programs and bootcamps for students and professionals in digital and cyber risk.

Innovation & Incubation

Developing new risk frameworks, AI governance models, and cybersecurity research.

Advisory Services

Helping organizations implement frameworks such as NIST, ISO 27001, and NIS2.

Community

IDR serves students, early-career professionals, and practitioners working in sectors like financial services and critical infrastructure. We foster an ecosystem where knowledge meets execution — through mentorship, events, and shared projects.
